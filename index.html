Fishing adventures

  • Little Red River

    • The Little Red River is my favorite river for for fishing brown trout. In early December the brown trout have a spawning run which creates excellent fishing conditions. I take a yearly trip there with a group of a guys

  • Pere Marquette River

    • The Pere Marquette River is one of the top rated fishery in the country. In the spring you can fish for Steelhead, in the summer you can fish for Brown Trout and in the fall you can fish for Salmon.


    Additional information
  • Little Sugar Creek

    • Also know as the Lil Sug, this little creek is great for bass fishing during the hottest months of the summer.
